Avatar The Last Airbender is an American animated series. It was co-created by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko. Avatar The Last Airbender takes place in a world of East Asian martial arts and elemental manipulation. The animated series mixes East Asian culture with Western culture, adding a new dimension to domestic cartoons in the United States. The animation currently has three seasons. Avatar The Last Airbender is set in a world made up of four nations named after four classical elements: Water Tribe, Earth Kingdom, Fire Nation, and Air Nomads. Some people in various countries will use their minds to display the elements of their country through Chinese martial arts. Only "Avatar" can cast all elements. "Avatar", as an international arbiter, has the responsibility of maintaining peace between nations and acting as a medium of communication between the human world and the underworld. When "Avatar" dies, his soul will be reincarnated in the order of fire, air, water, and earth. According to tradition, a newly born "Avatar" will travel the world, learn all the elemental martial arts, and then act as a global coordinator.
